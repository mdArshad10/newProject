SITE MEASURE

In home measure by an experienced designer. Discuss the custom design and function requirements specific to your space

Following your Showroom Consultation, one of our experienced designers will visit your home to measure your space, and record key details such as plumbing and electrical points, windows, and doors.

Our designers will discuss the scope of your project in detail, sharing their professional opinion on how best to bring your design to life, transforming your space with a custom design. They will discuss concepts and limitations, and agree on a brief to quote and draw.
